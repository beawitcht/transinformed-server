About

This service is for anyone living in the UK who is seeking gender affirming care from their GP, with a focus on gaining access to HRT. Currently the service is tailored to adults and we hope to create a similar resource for gender-diverse youth in the future.

This service allows you to generate a document to act as an aid when talking to your GP about accessing HRT.

This service can NOT guarantee that your GP will be co-operative in providing gender affirming care. This service also doesn't provide medical advice or encourage users to persue a particular pathway of care.

Currently, this service is tailored to England, with a plan to expand to all four nations of the UK in the future.

The best way to reach me is via twitter DMs, alternatively you can reach me at: contact@transinformed.co.uk

PDF generation is done through a third party API, as this service is run out of my own pocket, I can't always afford to pay for PDF availability. If you would like to contribute please reach out to me via email or twitter.
