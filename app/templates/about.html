About

This service is for anyone living in the UK who is seeking gender affirming care from their GP, with a focus on gaining access to HRT and other NHS services that a GP can facilitate.

A concise document that is tailored to your healthcare needs, that tries to make it as simple as possible for your GP to get the most important information to provide the best care, specifically highlighting the relevant guidance for transgender patients.

Unfortunately, this service can NOT guarantee that your GP will be co-operative in providing gender affirming care.

Currently, this service is tailored to England, with a plan to expand to all four nations of the UK in the future.

The best way to reach me is via twitter DMs, alternatively you can reach me at: contact@transinformed.co.uk
